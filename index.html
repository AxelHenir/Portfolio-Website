Alex Henri // Portfolio

>> Hi, I'm Alex!

I'm a CG Artist from Montreal, Canada. I specialize in 3D Modeling, Texturing, and Rendering for video, illustration and games.

I’m a 3D artist with a passion for creating visually compelling work that tells a story. Through experience across many projects, I’ve developed a strong foundation in both technical execution and creative problem-solving, allowing me to adapt quickly to different needs and visual styles.

My work spans product advertisement, environmental modeling, and object texturing, where I focus on balancing artistic vision with practical constraints. I enjoy breaking down challenges, refining details, and adding to my growing repertoire of techniques and skills.

I approach each project with intention and a thoughtful eye for form, material, and lighting. I bring a high level of attention to detail and a commitment to quality to everything I create. I’m motivated by the process of turning dreams into tangible media and continuously pushing myself to deliver work that is both technically sound and creatively engaging.

DigiLute (2024)

The DigiLute is a custom synthesizer controller. I designed and built it with Arduino and 3D printed parts.

Mox Amber (2024)

The Mox Amber was painted originally by Steve Belledin for the popular card game Magic: The Gathering. I was inspired to recreate it in a digital format using Blender.

The Secrets of Magic (2025)

The Secrets of Magic is a music playlist I made for my personal enjoyment and to share with others.

Game Over (2023)

A short typographic motion video listing off humanity's "score" as though its existence were a game.
