>> Overview

1 Million is a generative art making website made using the p5.js library. "1 Million" comes from the number of pixels in each piece as they are all 1000 px by 1000 px. On this site, I've created 5 pages where you can explore different generative models like grid recursion, lindenmeyer systems and circle packing. The site is meant to be an exploration tool focused on serendipidous outputs and play.
