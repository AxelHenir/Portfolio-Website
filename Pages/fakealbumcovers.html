>> Overview

Music is a very prevalent and present inspiration in much of my work and my life. When it isn't front and center, it's usually the backbone of my projects; either a major inspiration, motivation or output. I hope I'm not the only one who devises entire, fictitious realities where my playlists are the soundtrack. This fun hustle is how I developped my graphic art skills and how I continue to indulge to this day.

I prefer to model the scenes and situations in Blender. I use the built-in render engines to render out my album covers. I do this because being able to change camera angles, colors, textures, composition, etc. is far easier than in illustrated work, for example.

>> Disco Express

One of my favourite playlists is "Disco Express", a 30-minute, RnB/Jungle pick-me-up playlist featuring Montreal producer, KAYTRANADA. The album cover features the Toyota MK4 Supra, an icon in the automotive world. I used this car because of its prolific legacy in being an unbeatable force.
